About DemoKin

DemoKin is a comprehensive toolbox for the demographic analysis of kinship networks. It implements matrix-based models developed by Caswell and colleagues, allowing researchers to estimate the number and age distribution of relatives (kin) of a focal individual under various demographic assumptions.

The package supports both one-sex and two-sex models, time-invariant and time-varying approaches, and can incorporate additional dimensions like parity or educational attainment. DemoKin is designed to be flexible, efficient, and accessible to researchers in demography, sociology, and related fields.

Key Features

  • Estimate the expected number of living and deceased relatives by age
  • Analyze both ascending (parents, grandparents) and descending (children, grandchildren) kin
  • Incorporate sex-specific demographic rates
  • Model historical changes in demographic conditions
  • Include additional state variables beyond age
  • Visualize kinship networks and family structures
